About Us

      Islamic Center of Delaware County is a non-profit tax-exempt organization that has been formed exclusively for educational, religious, and social purposes. It is registered with the Internal Revenue Service under revenue code 501(c)(3). All of ICDC's activities are in accordance with the teachings of the Quran and following the teachings and traditions of Prophet Mohammed (Peace Be Upon Him).
